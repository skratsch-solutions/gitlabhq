GitLab tracks the resources your namespace consumes on GitLab.com, so you can monitor and manage
usage and storage before you reach a limit.

Usage refers to the resources a namespace and its projects consume, such as storage.

Storage refers to the disk space a project uses.
Each project has a storage limit that depends on your subscription tier.

When you monitor your usage, you can:

- Avoid unexpected restrictions on your projects.
- Plan storage purchases before you reach a limit.
- Make sure your team can continue to push code without interruption.
