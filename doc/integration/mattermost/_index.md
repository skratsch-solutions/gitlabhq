Mattermost bundled with the Linux package was
[deprecated in GitLab 18.9 and removed in GitLab 19.0](../../update/deprecations.md#mattermost-bundled-with-linux-package).

To migrate your bundled Mattermost to a standalone deployment, see
[Migrating from the bundled Mattermost to Mattermost Standalone](https://docs.mattermost.com/administration-guide/onboard/migrate-gitlab-omnibus.html)
in the Mattermost documentation.
